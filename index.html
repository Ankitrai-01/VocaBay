About VocaBay

VocaBay is an advanced voice-activated e-commerce ecosystem built to explore hands-free shopping. We merge high-fidelity continuous speech-parsing NLP engines with premium cyber dark visual systems.

AI Voice Assistant

Fully hands-free web control utilizing dynamic AI voice-parsing. Navigate, search, select options, and place transactions entirely by speaking.

Multi-Role Framework

Architected to seamlessly support Buyers (tracking live orders), Vendors (managing stores and listings), and Administrators (approving accounts).

Persistent Database

Powered by a secure Express/Node.js backend connected to a MongoDB persistent storage cluster for reliable accounts, dynamic catalog products, and orders tracking.

Voice Guide

Navigation
"go to home" / "open home" Redirection to home page
"go to shop" / "open shop" Browse product collections
"go to cart" / "view cart" Check your cart items
"go to checkout" Open secure checkout view
"scroll down" / "scroll up" Smoothly navigate long pages
Discovery
"search for [product]" Search catalog by keyword
"open product [name]" Show detailed specifications
Cart & Purchase
"select size [small/medium/large]" Choose product sizes (S, M, L, XL)
"add to cart" / "add this" Add item to shopping cart
"select cash on delivery" Set payment method to COD
"select razorpay" / "card" Set payment method to Razorpay
"place order" / "confirm order" Submit details and complete purchase
